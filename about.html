About Me

Now I am a 1st year advance research engineer at Midea Building Technology Institute, aimed at integrating HVAC with Machine Learning. I'm interested in automatic modeling, calibration and optimization of HVAC, building load forecasting, and fusion of Knowledge Graph and Computing Graph. I am co-advised by Lin Yanqing and Meng Tao.

Prior to my work, I received my B.E. degree in BEEE(Building Environment and Energy Engineering) and Master degree in HVAC(Heating, Gas Supply, Ventilating and Air Conditioning Engineering) at Tongji University in ShangHai (2015-2022). I was privileged to be advised by Prof. HongWei Tan.

In the near future, I wish I can apply machine learning to more areas besides HVAC, such as finance, healthcare, energy, agriculture, education, transportation and so on. If you share the same vision as me, please don't hesitate to contact me.
