About Me

Now I am a 1st year advance research engineer at Midea Building Technology Institute, aimed at integrating industry knowledge with data intelligence. I'm interested in automatic modeling, calibration and optimization of HVAC, building load forecasting, fusion of Knowledge Graph and Computing Graph, and Machine Learning in HVAC. I am co-advised by Lin Yanqing and Meng Tao.

Prior to my work, I received my B.E. degree in BEEE(Building Environment and Energy Engineering) and Master degree in HVAC(Heating, Gas Supply, Ventilating and Air Conditioning Engineering) at Tongji University in shanghai (2015-2022). I was privileged to be advised by HongWei Tan.
